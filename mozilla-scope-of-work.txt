Scope/Statement of Work

A half-time instructional designer (to be hired) will create, publish,
and update teaching materials for workshops aimed specifically at
undergraduate students.

An administrative assistant at Mozilla will coordinate workshops and
instructors, e.g., pair instructors with workshops at sites other than
the main five, arrange travel, etc.)

Ms. Kaitlin Thaney will supervise the administrative staff at Mozilla,
and will be responsible for connecting the other PIs and the graduate
student assistants with other open and web-enabled science groups.

Dr. Greg Wilson will provide instructional training for the graduate
student assistants and other participants in the project on an ongoing
basis, and will be responsible for organizing the workshops aimed at
students from underrepresented minorities.  He will also collaborate
with the instructional designer on development of teaching materials.
